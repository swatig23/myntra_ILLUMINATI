Services

Mask Detection

Our state of the art Real Time Mask Detection Algorithm, can detect whether a person is wearing a mask or not within seconds with 99% accuracy.

Fullfilling one of the basic requirements of COVID precautions, our glove recognition algorithm helps detect medical grade gloves on the hands of people in real time.
